Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How far in advance should I order?
Standard orders need 24 hours notice. Custom cakes and large event orders require 3–5 days. For weddings, 2–3 weeks is recommended.
Do you deliver outside Hyderabad?
We deliver across Hyderabad. For outstation shipping, dry items like cookies, barfi, and ladoos can be shipped pan-India. Fresh cakes are Hyderabad-only.
Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Yes! We offer eggless, sugar-free, and gluten-aware options for most items. Please mention your requirements when ordering or contacting us.
Do you do corporate or bulk orders?
Absolutely. We have dedicated corporate pricing for orders of 50+ units. Contact us with your requirements for a custom quote and branding options.
What payment methods do you accept?
UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery for local orders. A 50% advance is required for custom cakes above ₹2,000.
Can I pick up my order from the studio?
Yes — studio pickup from our Jubilee Hills location is available during all opening hours and is free of charge.
